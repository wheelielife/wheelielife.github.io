About Wheelie Life

Wheelie Life is a free online stunt game that lets you perform thrilling wheelies on a bike across a smooth cityscape. The game offers realistic physics and responsive controls, giving you a taste of the stunt rider's life without leaving your screen. It's the perfect game for adrenaline lovers and those who enjoy bike games with smooth graphics and fun challenges.

How to play Wheelie Life

In Wheelie Life, you ride through a 3D cityscape, pulling off wheelies while avoiding crashes. Your main goal is to keep your bike's front wheel in the air for as long as possible. Simple in concept but addictive in practice, this game tests your timing, balance, and reflexes.
Try to keep your bike balanced on the track and sprint towards the finish line. Make sure your speed will reach the highest score.

Control: Use the left mouse button to click, hold, and release your bike to keep it balanced.

Tips to Win in Wheelie Life

  • Keep the right balance between speed and control.
  • Don't lift the wheel too high, or you will flip.
  • Upgrade your speed, income and power packs.
  • Practice short balance driving sessions before taking on longer trips.

Wheelie Life is a free online game that is fun, fast-paced and suitable for all players. The game challenges all skill levels and quick reflexes to get higher wheels and get the best score.
